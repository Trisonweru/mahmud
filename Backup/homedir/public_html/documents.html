Required Documents

What you need to apply.

Have these ready before you start so we can review your application quickly.

Valid Passport

Valid for at least 6 months from your arrival date, with two blank pages.

Digital Photo

A recent passport-style photo on a clear background, taken within the last 6 months.

Flight Ticket

A confirmed return or onward ticket showing your travel dates.

Proof of Accommodation

Hotel reservation or invitation letter from your host in Somalia.

Travel Dates

Clear arrival and departure dates that match your itinerary.

Payment Method

A credit or debit card to complete the $94 processing fee.
