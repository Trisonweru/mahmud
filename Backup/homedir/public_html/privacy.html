Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PassKey Technologies Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and related visa assistance services.

1. Data Controller

PassKey Technologies Ltd — a private company registered in Kenya. We are an independent third-party service provider and are not affiliated with the Government of Somalia.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: Full Name, Date of Birth, Nationality
  • Passport Details: Passport Number, Country, Issue & Expiry Date
  • Special Category / Biometric Data: Passport Photo / Scan
  • Contact Information: Email Address, Phone Number
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type

3. How We Use Your Data

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2018:

  • Contract Performance: To prepare and submit your eTAS application.
  • Explicit Consent: For processing your passport photo (biometric data).
  • Legitimate Interests: Customer support, fraud prevention, service improvement.

4. International Data Transfers

Your application data will be transferred to the Government of Somalia (etas.gov.so) for processing your eTAS application.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

  • The Government of Somalia (etas.gov.so)
  • Our payment gateway provider
  • Trusted service providers under strict confidentiality

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data.

6. Data Retention

  • Passport images and application documents: deleted within 7 days after submission
  • Transaction records: retained for up to 7 years as required by Kenyan law

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, request deletion of, object to processing, or withdraw consent for your data. Contact support@evisasomali.com.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies only. We do not use non-essential tracking cookies without your consent.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
