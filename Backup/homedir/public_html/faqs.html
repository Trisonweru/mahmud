FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Answers to the questions our applicants ask most often.

How long does the application take?
Most applications are reviewed within 72 hours. Expedited processing is available for urgent travel.
Is this the official Somalia government website?
No. We are an independent service that supports your visa application. The official portal is etas.gov.so.
How much does the eVisa cost?
Our service fee is $94 USD per application — single entry, valid for up to 180 days.
What happens if my application is denied?
We will reach out to explain the reason and guide you through corrections or next steps.
Is my personal data secure?
Yes. All data is transmitted using bank-grade encryption and is never shared with third parties.
I'm a member of the Somali diaspora — how do I apply?
Simple — send us your passport, photo, WhatsApp number and email. We will prepare the entire application for you.
